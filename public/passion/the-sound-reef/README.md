# The Sound Reef

Drop your music files here to grow the reef.

## Structure
- `instruments/guitar/` — Guitar tabs, recordings, learning materials
- `instruments/piano/` — Piano sheets, performances
- `instruments/drums/` — Drum patterns, practice recordings
- `instruments/composition/` — Original compositions, DAW projects
- `scores/` — Sheet music
- `videos/` — Performance videos
- `theory/` — Music theory notes
- `performances/` — Live performance records
- `albums/` — Album covers, reviews, collection logs
- `playlists/` — Mood playlists, curated collections

**Supported**: JPG, PNG, PDF, MP4, MP3, TXT, MD
