# Motion Garden

Drop your art here and watch the garden bloom.

## Structure
- `painting/` — Paintings, digital art, illustrations
- `photography/` — Photography collections
- `dance/` — Dance videos, choreography notes
- `inspiration-wall/` — Mood boards, references, inspiration
- `sketches/` — Sketchbook scans, quick drawings

**Supported**: JPG, PNG, MP4, TXT, MD
